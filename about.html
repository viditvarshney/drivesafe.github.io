Strong Roots About System

In order to ensure pedestrian safety and vehicle speed limits in areas with high pedestrian density/ Residential Areas, a system to detect the traffic density/ School Areas/ Residential Areas and accordingly warn the driver about the speed limit sighns or sign boards for school/hospital/ accident prone areas etc. and may decide upon the safe speed value with which the vehicle need to operate.

The system needs to provide a warning to the driver, if the speed limit are breached. For the purpose of demonstration, the warning may be provided using a LCD or a seven segment simple display and a buzzer. The sign board for the purpose of demonstration needs to be made according to IRC standards and the same can be used by the system for demonstration purpose
